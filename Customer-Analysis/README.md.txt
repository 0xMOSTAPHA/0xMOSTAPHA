📊 Customer Analytics Power BI Dashboard
A multi-page interactive business intelligence dashboard built with Power BI, providing a 360° view of customer behavior, marketing performance, and revenue trends across 2013–2018.
📌 Pages
1. Customers Analysis
Overview of 15K customers across demographics — top countries, regions, states, income levels, gender distribution, and age groups.
2. Events & Offers Analysis
Breakdown of 307K events and 168K offers — offer types (BOGO, discount, informational), completion rates, rewards earned, and age-based engagement.
3. Income Analysis
Deep dive into $970M total revenue — income by gender, age class, region, country, and year-over-year growth trends.
✨ Features

Dark-themed, visually cohesive UI with consistent pink/purple color palette
KPI cards, donut charts, bar charts, area charts, and data tables
Year and quarter/month slicers for dynamic filtering
Comparative gender analysis across all dimensions

🛠️ Tools & Tech

Power BI Desktop
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
Data modeling with relationships across customer, transaction, and offer tables

📁 Dataset
Simulated retail/loyalty program dataset including customer demographics, transaction history, and marketing campaign responses.
